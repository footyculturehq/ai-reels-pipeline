# Footy Culture HQ — Brand Brief

## Channel
**@footyculturehq** — Football boot resell & flip intelligence.
Core value: tell people *which boots to buy, when to buy, when to sell* for profit.
Discord: 75+ members. Paid Pro tier = instant bot alerts on drops/restocks.

## Audience
- 16–28 year olds, football fans
- Boot collectors, flippers, players wanting limited colourways at retail
- Sneaker culture applied to football boots

## Market
- Global boot resell market: $4.2B+
- Limited boots sell 30–40% above retail (StockX, eBay, GOAT, Vinted)
- Drops sell out in <20 minutes — most people miss them
- Pro members get bot alerts before it hits social media

## Key Brands & Boots
- **Nike**: Phantom GX, Mercurial Superfly/Vapor, Tiempo Legend, Air Zoom Mercurial
- **Adidas**: Predator Elite/Pro, Copa Pure, X Crazyfast
- **New Balance**: Furon, Tekela
- **Puma**: Future Ultimate, King Platinum, Ultra Ultimate
- **Mizuno**: Morelia Neo (highly collectible)
- **Special editions**: Player PEs, World Cup colourways, limited club colourways, anniversary packs

- Confident, data-driven, street-smart
- Like getting alpha from a mate who knows the market
- Real numbers (buy/sell prices, % gains, timeframes)
- Short and punchy — hook in first 2 seconds, deliver value fast
